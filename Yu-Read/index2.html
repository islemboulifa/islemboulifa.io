Motivation ?

A literature club, that's where the idea originated from, but it wasn't the main reason.

The first and obvious one is: I love books and reading, so a personal library has always been on my mind. Secondly, I wanted to make something bigger than anything I have ever did before, at least for me. The idea was simple, though, building a website from scratch was no easy task, Still, I believe it was an excellent exercise and a memorable life experience. I've had the chance to learn a lot of php, totally new implementations of JavaScript, and test my HTML CSS knowledge.

At the end, and after everything came together, worked as intended, it honestly gave me great pleasure, you could even say, a sense of accomplishment.
