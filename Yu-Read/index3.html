Tutorial

Dedicated to those of you, who couldn't navigate

1. Register

Fill in the registration form to create a new account. When done press Enter or click the Register button.

2. Login

After you have successfully created an account, you'd be redirected to the login page Where you can write your new account details and get access to the site. Press Enter or Click on Login once finished.

3. Navigation

You'd be welcomed to the Home page. From there, you could either logout or Head to your Yu-Read library by clicking on the Yu-Books Button from the navigation bar.

4. Your Library

You decided to go to your library and start stuffing it with your Books and Manga or what ever. Initially, i intended to make it cool 3d graphical library, it was too much for my first serious project, so regrettably, i gave up on the idea. Sorry. Here you gonna find the all the titles you've added to your precious library. To add or remove an Item from your Library click on the Book icon.

5. Library Editor

We arrive at the Library Editor. You can either add an item, through filling the form with data; such as title, author, rating and the statue (Wishlist: Not owened yet. Reading: still unfinished. Finished: you could give it to your crush now.)
